The embedded SGB1 / SGB2 GB-side boot ROMs in sgb/sgb.cpp
(`kSgbBootRom` and `kSgb2BootRom`, 256 bytes each) are clean-room
replacements written by Lior Halphon for the SameBoy project, and are
distributed under the Expat (MIT) License reproduced below.

Source:    https://github.com/LIJI32/SameBoy/tree/master/BootROMs
Routed via https://github.com/SourMesen/Mesen2 (Core/Gameboy/GbBootRom.h)
           which embeds the same SameBoy bytes (with a small SGB delay
           loop tweak at the handoff that we also carry).
